Project Details - Adobe Illustrator

C257 GA - Website

In this module, I was tasked to design a website using Adobe Illustrator, utilisng the knowledge I have gained in the module, such as Design Principles, Gestalt Principles, LinkedIN courses showcasing the tools from the applications of Adobe PhotoShop and Adobe Illustrator.

Designing this specific website, I thought about my colour combinations, and what could possibly represent me and give out the message that I want. Using purple as a colour, I discovered that purple represents royalty and rarity, magic and mystery. As a gaming company (made-up), I want to show our consumers that the products are interesting and cool.
