Project Details - Figma

C218 CA - Jin Eats. Co

In this module, I was tasked to create a mobile app design, after learning more about figma as well as some design concepts. This is the application of the skills learnt through LinkedIN courses.

When designing this mobile application, I thought about how a food delivery app works, and what key features there are, conducting the UX research process of Ideation, Research, Design. Coming up with the colours are difficult, and settling on red and yellow was due to it being more vibrant and attractive colours.
